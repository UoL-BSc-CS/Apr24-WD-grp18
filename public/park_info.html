Having a Safe Visit

Safety is our top priority in our park. Although we strive to provide a safe experience as much as possible, we advise our visitors to follow some guidelines for a comfortable visit.

  • Do not leave your kids unattended except for designated playgrounds.
  • Keep your belongings with you all the time.
  • Make sure to see your doctor before your visit if you have any health issues.
